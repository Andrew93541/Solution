Marketplace Guidelines

To maintain a sustainable and trustworthy marketplace, please follow these guidelines

Product Requirements

  • All products must be eco-friendly and sustainable
  • Products should have minimal environmental impact
  • Clear, high-quality images are required for all listings
  • Accurate product descriptions and pricing are mandatory
  • Products must be in good condition and fully functional

Seller Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate contact information
  • Respond to buyer inquiries within 24 hours
  • Ship products in eco-friendly packaging
  • Maintain transparent communication about shipping times
  • Handle returns and refunds professionally

Community Standards

  • Treat all members with respect
  • Provide honest product reviews and ratings
  • Report any suspicious activity
  • Share sustainable practices and tips
  • Support local and small businesses

Prohibited Items

  • Non-eco-friendly products
  • Products with excessive packaging
  • Items containing harmful chemicals
  • Counterfeit or unauthorized products
  • Products that violate environmental regulations

By using our marketplace, you agree to follow these guidelines. Violations may result in account suspension or removal of listings.
